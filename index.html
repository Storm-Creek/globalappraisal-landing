SMS Communications Policy

Global Appraisal may send text messages to property owners, their representatives, and real estate agents for the purpose of scheduling and confirming real estate appraisal inspections.

  • Messages are limited to appointment scheduling, confirmation, reminders, and coordination.
  • Message frequency varies; typically 1–5 messages per appraisal engagement.
  • Message and data rates may apply.

Consent: By providing your phone number as part of the appraisal engagement process—whether through a lender, mortgage company, real estate agent, or directly—you consent to receive appointment-related text messages from Global Appraisal.

Opt-out: Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance. You may also call (919) 328-0141 or email edwin@globalappraisal.net to opt out.
